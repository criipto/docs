## Integrate Idura Verify

Idura Verify can be integrated with any tech stack using the standard [OpenID Connect (OIDC) protocol](/verify/getting-started/oidc-intro/).
You don't need to be an identity expert to integrate Idura: a trusted OIDC client library or one of our dedicated SDKs will handle most of the complexity for you.
Idura can also easily connect to your existing CIAM system, such as Auth0, AWS Cognito, or Firebase.

<VerifyIntegrationCards />

## Integrate Idura Signatures

Idura Signatures is designed to be embedded directly into your application.
[Using our GraphQL API](/signatures/getting-started/), you can build custom signing workflows that feel native to your app and are tailored to your exact needs.
You can leverage GraphQL code generators for your preferred stack or use one of our SDKs for select platforms.

## Supported eIDs

Idura Verify supports authentication via a number of national eIDs across Europe, including Danish MitID, Norwegian and Swedish BankID, German Personalausweis, and more.
